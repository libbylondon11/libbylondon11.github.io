Northern Botanics (previously Born and Dyed in MN) was founded in 2014 by Libby London. Combining her training as a papermaker and book artist, and sustainable farmer and designer, Northern Botanics cultivates plants for natural dye and fiber. Located in Minnesota, growing and foraging regionally sustainable plants is at the forefront of our mission and work.
